Our Goal

Monitoring your recommended daily calorie intake helps keep track of your body’s maintenance, and can even help in weight reduction!

How to use the App:

First, enter your gender, height, weight, age, and your activity level. This information will help in determining the right calorie intake unique for each person. Press “calculate” to view your recommended daily calorie intake.
